About


KSI, whose real name is Olajide Olatunji, is a British YouTuber, rapper, and boxer. He first gained fame on YouTube for his FIFA gameplay videos and commentary, and later expanded into vlogging, comedy skits, and music. He has amassed millions of followers across his various social media platforms, and has also released several successful singles and albums. In addition to his online career, KSI has also dabbled in the world of professional boxing, having competed in several high-profile matches, including a highly publicized rematch with fellow YouTuber Logan Paul. Overall, KSI is a multi-talented individual who has made a name for himself in the world of entertainment through his creativity, wit, and ability to connect with his audience.
